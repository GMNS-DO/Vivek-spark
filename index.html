💡  Tips to Move Forward
1
Have the Career Conversation Early
Don’t wait for your annual review. Schedule 30 minutes with your manager this month — just about your future, not your current tasks.
2
Build Visibility, Not Just Skills
Skills get you ready. Visibility gets you considered. Volunteer for cross-functional work and let the right people see what you can do.
3
Update Your Internal Profile
It’s often the first thing a hiring manager sees. Reflect your current experience, aspirations, and wins — not just your job title.
